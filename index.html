About Our Church

Shalom Covenant Church Arua is a vibrant community of believers committed to spreading the Gospel and serving our community.

APOSTLE JEMA JOHN

Senior Pastor

Apostle Jema John has been serving as the Senior Pastor of Shalom Covenant Church Arua since its founding. With a passion for teaching God's Word and a heart for the community, he leads our congregation with wisdom and compassion.

Under his leadership, the church has grown both spiritually and numerically, impacting countless lives in Arua and beyond.

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who worship passionately, love extravagantly, and witness boldly.

Our Vision

To be a transforming presence in Arua through the power of the Gospel, impacting generations for Christ.

Our Values

Biblical truth, authentic worship, genuine community, compassionate service, and intentional discipleship.
